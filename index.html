Sound Effects Generator

This tool generates sound effects for the Library Mystery Game using the Web Audio API. You can:

  • Play individual sounds to preview them
  • Download individual sounds as MP3 files
  • Generate and download all sounds at once

After downloading, place the sound files in your /static/sounds/ directory.
